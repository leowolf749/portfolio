about

My name is Leo. Ever since I can remember I've been creating. I've always felt compelled to bring abstract ideas into fruition. As a Junior Developer specializing in front-end engineering I am able to do just that. I am a recent graduate from a 12 week immersive program at The Iron Yard in Charlotte, NC, where I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript,frameworks including AngularJS and BackboneJS. I also gained experience using JavaScript libraries and other various build tools (AJAX, SASS, Foundation, Gulp, Git, Github). When I'm not writing code I like to spend my time working on music and art projects, reading, playing sports and video games, or doing any outdoor activity.
